<p><p>The multibeam sonar market encompasses three different frequencies: low, medium, and high. Low frequency sonar is ideal for deep-water environments as it offers long-range coverage but lacks fine detail. Medium frequency sonar provides a balance between range and resolution, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. High frequency sonar offers the highest resolution but limited range, making it more effective for shallow-water operations. Each frequency has its own advantages and considerations, allowing end-users to select the most appropriate sonar system based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
