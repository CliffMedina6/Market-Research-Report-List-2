<p><p>The food supplement ingredients market consists of various types, namely powder, gel, liquid, capsules, and tablets. Powder supplements are finely-ground substances that can be mixed with food or beverages. Gel supplements are typically in a semi-solid form, easily absorbed by the body. Liquid supplements are in a fluid state, often consumed directly or added to drinks. Capsules are small containers enclosing powdered or liquid ingredients. Tablets are solid, compressed forms that dissolve in the stomach. These different types cater to diverse consumer preferences and provide convenient ways to consume food supplements.</p></p>
