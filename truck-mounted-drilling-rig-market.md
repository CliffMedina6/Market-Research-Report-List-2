<p><p>The truck-mounted drilling rig market is categorized into three types: hydraulic pressure, pneumatic, and power-driven. Hydraulic pressure rigs utilize fluid mechanics to generate intense pressure for drilling operations, offering high power and efficiency. Pneumatic rigs, on the other hand, employ compressed air systems to create force, providing excellent mobility and versatility. Power-driven rigs use engines or motors to provide the necessary force for drilling, allowing for greater control and customization. These different types cater to varying drilling needs and preferences, ensuring a wide range of options in the truck-mounted drilling rig market.</p></p>
