<p><p>Electromagnetic Wave Absorbers are used to absorb or reduce the impact of electromagnetic waves in various applications. Resonant absorbers are designed to absorb specific frequencies and work by using a material with a resonant frequency that matches the desired wave frequency. Broadband absorbers, on the other hand, are designed to absorb a wide range of frequencies. They typically consist of a material with properties that allow it to attenuate a wide spectrum of electromagnetic waves. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the electromagnetic wave absorber market.</p></p>
