<p><p>A Skeleton Oil Seal, also known as a mechanical face seal or a floating seal, is primarily used to prevent the leakage of lubricants in various rotating machinery applications. It consists of two identical sealing rings, typically made of metal (such as steel) or ceramics, that face each other and are separated by a small gap filled with lubricating fluid. This design allows the seal to compensate for any misalignment or runout between the rotating and stationary components.</p><p>The Skeleton Oil Seal Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
