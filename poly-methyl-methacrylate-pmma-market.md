Additionally, the construction sector is also witnessing growth, with PMMA being utilized in windows, doors, and skylights due to its transparency and high impact resistance.</p><p>Furthermore, the electronics industry is another major consumer of PMMA, with applications in displays, lighting, and optical fibers.
