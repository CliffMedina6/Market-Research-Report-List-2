<p><p>The Saw Blade Cutter market is categorized into three types based on the type of teeth: Coarse Tooth, Middle Tooth, and Fine Tooth. Coarse Tooth blades have larger teeth with widely spaced gaps, making them ideal for cutting through thick and rough materials. Middle Tooth blades have medium-sized teeth that are versatile and can be used for various cutting tasks. Fine Tooth blades have smaller teeth closely spaced together, providing smooth and clean cuts for delicate materials. These different market types cater to varying cutting needs and enable users to select blades based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
