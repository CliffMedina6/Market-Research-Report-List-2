<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) Pipe is a type of plastic pipe that has undergone a cross-linking process, which improves its strength and durability. There are three main types of PE-X Pipe: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are produced using the peroxide method, resulting in excellent high-temperature performance and flexibility. PE-Xb pipes are manufactured through the silane method, offering good resistance to stress cracking and durability. Lastly, PE-Xc pipes are made via the electron beam method, delivering exceptional strength and resistance to chlorine-based chemicals. These different types cater to specific applications and usage requirements in the market.</p></p>
