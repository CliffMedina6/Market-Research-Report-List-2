<p><ul><li>Physical Method</li><li>Chemical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powdered activated carbon market can be classified into two types: physical method and chemical method. In the physical method, activated carbon is produced by crushing and grinding carbonaceous materials, such as wood or coal, into a fine powder. In the chemical method, carbonaceous materials are chemically activated using chemicals like phosphoric acid or zinc chloride. Both methods result in powdered activated carbon, which is widely used in various applications such as water purification, air filtration, and industrial processing for its adsorptive properties.</p></p>
