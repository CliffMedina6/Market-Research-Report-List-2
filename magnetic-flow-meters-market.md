<p><p>Magnetic flow meters, also known as mag meters or electromagnetic flow meters, are devices used to measure the flow rate of conductive liquids. They work based on Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction, which states that when a conductive liquid flows through a magnetic field, a voltage is generated that is proportional to the flow velocity. These meters are widely used in various industries such as water and wastewater management, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The future outlook for the magnetic flow meters market is highly positive.
