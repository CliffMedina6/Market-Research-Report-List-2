<p><p>The fiber optic components market consists of various types of components used in fiber optic communication systems. Fiber optic transceivers are devices that transmit and receive data over fiber optic cables. Fiber optic switches are used to route data between multiple fiber optic cables. Fiber optic connectors are used to connect fiber optic cables together. Fiber optic couplers combine light from multiple fibers into one. Fiber optic amplifiers boost the strength of optical signals. Optical power splitters divide optical signals into multiple paths. Optical attenuators reduce the intensity of optical signals. Optical circulators direct the flow of light in a specific direction. Fiber optic lasers emit coherent light for various applications.</p></p>
