<p><p>RF Front-end (RFFE) Filters market comprises of Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Filters. SAW Filters are electronic components that use acoustic waves to filter and clean the transmitted signals in various wireless devices. They are cost-effective and suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, BAW Filters utilize a piezoelectric transducer to generate acoustic waves, making them ideal for filtering out unwanted signals in advanced wireless communication systems. Both SAW and BAW Filters play a crucial role in enhancing signal quality and reducing interference in RF front-end circuits.</p></p>
