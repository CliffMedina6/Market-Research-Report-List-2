<p><p>The oil pump market consists of two main types: Electric Oil Pump and Mechanical Oil Pump. An Electric Oil Pump is powered by an electric motor, providing efficient and controlled oil flow, which makes it suitable for modern vehicles with advanced engine management systems. On the other hand, a Mechanical Oil Pump is driven directly by the engine's mechanical system, relying on the engine's rotation to pump oil throughout the vehicle. Mechanical oil pumps are commonly found in older or simpler engine models. Both types serve the purpose of circulating oil to lubricate and cool the engine components.</p></p>
