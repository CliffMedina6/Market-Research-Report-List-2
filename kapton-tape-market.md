<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Kapton Tape market consists of two main types: Silicone Based Polyimide Tape and Acrylic Based Polyimide Tape. The Silicone Based Polyimide Tape is made by applying a silicone adhesive on a polyimide film. It offers strong adhesion, high temperature resistance, and excellent electrical insulation properties, making it suitable for various industries like electronics, aerospace, and automotive. On the other hand, Acrylic Based Polyimide Tape has an acrylic adhesive applied on a polyimide film. It provides good adhesion and thermal resistance, making it commonly used in masking, insulation, and splicing applications.</p></p>
