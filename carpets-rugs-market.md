<p><p>The carpets and rugs market can be broadly divided into different types: tufted, woven, needle-punched, knotted, and others. Tufted carpets are manufactured by tufting yarns into a backing material, while woven carpets are made on looms using interlacing techniques. Needle-punched carpets involve punching fibers through a base fabric, and knotted carpets are created by tying knots in the yarns. The "others" category includes various methods like hand-hooked, flat-weave, and embroidered carpets. Each type offers different textures, designs, and durability, catering to diverse consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
