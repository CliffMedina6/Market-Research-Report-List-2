The blow molding machines are used to create hollow plastic products by inflating hot plastic tubes into molds. Extrusion machinery is used to produce continuous plastic profiles, sheets, or pipes by melting plastic and forcing it through a die. Injection molding machines are used to inject molten plastic material into molds to create various plastic products. Other types of machines include thermoforming, rotomolding, and compounding machines that are used for specific plastic processing purposes.</p></p>
