<p><strong>The Protective Packaging for Shipping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Systems</li><li>Passive Systems</li><li>Hybrid Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protective packaging for shipping is categorized into three market types: active systems, passive systems, and hybrid systems. </p><p>Active systems refer to packaging solutions that actively control the shipping environment by utilizing technologies like temperature or humidity control devices to maintain predetermined conditions. Passive systems, on the other hand, are packaging materials that provide insulation and protection without relying on external technologies. Lastly, hybrid systems combine both active and passive components to ensure optimum protection during shipping, utilizing elements like insulation with temperature control devices for enhanced safety. These different market types cater to various shipping needs and ensure the safe transportation of goods.</p></p>
