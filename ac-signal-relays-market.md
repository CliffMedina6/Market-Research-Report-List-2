<p><ul><li>TH Mounting</li><li>SMT Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC signal relays are electronic devices used to control the flow of alternating current signals in electrical systems. They come in two types of mounting: TH (Through-Hole) and SMT (Surface Mount Technology). </p><p>TH mounting involves inserting the relay's terminals into pre-drilled holes on a PCB (Printed Circuit Board) and then soldering them. This method offers better mechanical stability and higher power handling capabilities.</p><p>SMT mounting, on the other hand, involves soldering the relay directly onto the surface of the PCB. It offers increased space efficiency and is ideal for high-density electronic assemblies.</p></p>
