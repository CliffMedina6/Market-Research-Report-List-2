<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Boiler Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modulating Control</li><li>On/Off Control</li><li>High-fire/low-fire Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boiler Control market consists of three main types: Modulating Control, On/Off Control, and High-fire/Low-fire Control. Modulating Control allows for precise regulation of the boiler's output in response to varying heating demands. On/Off Control operates the boiler in a binary fashion, either fully on or fully off, based on specific temperature thresholds. High-fire/Low-fire Control adjusts the firing rate of the boiler between high and low levels, depending on the required heat output. These market segments cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in efficiently managing boiler operations.</p></p>
