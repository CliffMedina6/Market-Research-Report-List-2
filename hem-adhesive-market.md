<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hem Adhesive Market consists of two primary types: One Component and Two Component. One Component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, allowing for easy application without the need for mixing. They are typically used for simple applications and have a shorter shelf life. On the other hand, Two Component adhesives require mixing two different components, usually a resin and a hardener, before application. They offer greater bonding strength and are commonly used in more complex and demanding applications.</p></p>
