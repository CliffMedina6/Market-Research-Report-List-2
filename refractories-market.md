<p><p>The refractories market includes both shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are pre-formed products, such as bricks, tiles, and blocks, which are used to line the walls, floors, and roofs of high-temperature industrial equipment. They provide insulation and protection against heat and chemical corrosion. On the other hand, unshaped refractories include materials like castables, rams, and monolithics, which are designed to be installed in a liquid or semi-liquid state. They are used to fill gaps, repair linings, and create custom shapes, providing flexibility and adaptability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
