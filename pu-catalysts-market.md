Reactive amine catalysts are substances that actively participate in the chemical reaction, promoting the PU formation. They enable faster curing and improve mechanical properties. Non-reactive amine catalysts, on the other hand, do not actively take part in the reaction but enhance the reaction rate by facilitating the reaction conditions. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in the PU industry by ensuring optimal performance and properties in the final product.</p></p>
