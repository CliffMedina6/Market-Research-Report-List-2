<p><p>Vulcanizers are essential tools used in the rubber industry for curing and shaping rubber products. There are two main types of vulcanizers on the market: those with a rotor and those without a rotor, known as rotorless vulcanizers. Vulcanizers with a rotor include a rotating mechanism that helps evenly distribute the heat during the vulcanization process. On the other hand, rotorless vulcanizers rely on other methods to achieve uniform heating, such as the use of hot air circulation or electric heating elements. Both types of vulcanizers serve the same purpose but differ in their heating mechanisms.</p></p>
