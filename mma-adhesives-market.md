<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The MMA Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Adhesives</li><li>Thermoplasticity Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MMA adhesives, also known as Methyl Methacrylate adhesives, are categorized into two main types: Thermosetting adhesives and Thermoplasticity adhesives. </p><p>Thermosetting adhesives, when cured or hardened, form irreversible chemical bonds, providing high strength and durability. They are commonly used in applications where heat resistance, chemical resistance, and structural integrity are crucial.</p><p>Thermoplasticity adhesives, on the other hand, soften and re-harden when heated, allowing them to be reshaped. They offer excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for applications that require bonding of dissimilar materials and adaptability to varying temperatures.</p><p>Both types of MMA adhesives play vital roles in a wide range of industries, including automotive, construction, electronics, and aerospace.</p></p>
