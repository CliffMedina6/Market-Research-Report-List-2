<p><p>The Ultrasonic Motion Sensor market consists of two main types: Proximity Detection and Ranging Measurement. Proximity Detection sensors are used to detect the presence or absence of an object within a specific range, helping automate tasks like turning on lights when someone enters a room. On the other hand, Ranging Measurement sensors are designed to measure the distance between the sensor and an object accurately. These sensors are commonly used in industries like robotics, automotive, and aerospace for navigation, collision avoidance, and object detection purposes.</p></p>
