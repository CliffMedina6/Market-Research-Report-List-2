<p><p>Specialty packaging paper is divided into two types based on the material used: wood pulp fiber and kraft paper. Wood pulp fiber material is made from the pulp of trees and is known for its high strength and durability. It is commonly used in packaging materials such as corrugated boxes. On the other hand, kraft paper is made from the chemical pulp of wood and is known for its high tear resistance and smooth finish. It is often used in specialty packaging for products like food, beverages, and consumer goods.</p></p>
