<p><p>The Piezo Ceramic market consists of three main types: Lead zinc titanates (PZT), Lead titanate (PT), and Lead magnesium niobate (PMN). Lead zinc titanates are widely used due to their excellent piezoelectric properties and high Curie temperature. Lead titanate is notable for its high electromechanical coupling factor, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. Lead magnesium niobate exhibits exceptional dielectric and piezoelectric properties, making it ideal for actuators and sensors. These different types of piezo ceramics cater to various industries, such as automotive, healthcare, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
