<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) curing coatings market comprises two major types: water-based coatings and powder-based coatings. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent and are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) emissions. These coatings are commonly used in applications where a high-quality finish is desired, such as automotive and furniture industries. On the other hand, powder-based coatings are solvent-free and are applied in a dry powder form. They produce a tough and durable finish, making them suitable for metal surfaces, appliances, and outdoor furniture.</p></p>
