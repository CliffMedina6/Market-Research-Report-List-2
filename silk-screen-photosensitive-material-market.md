This material is applied onto the mesh screen, also known as a stencil, and exposed to light to create an image or design. The areas that are exposed to light harden, allowing ink to pass through, while the unexposed areas are washed away, preventing ink from passing through. <p><p>The silk screen photosensitive material market includes two main types: photosensitive glue and photosensitive film. Photosensitive glue is a type of material that is sensitive to light and undergoes a chemical reaction when exposed, making it suitable for creating intricate designs on various substrates using silk screen printing. On the other hand, photosensitive film is a thin layer of material that also reacts to light exposure but comes in pre-made sheets, making it easier to use for creating detailed patterns on different surfaces, including textiles, glass, and ceramics.</p></p>
