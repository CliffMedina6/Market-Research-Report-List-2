<p><p>Fuse holders are essential components used to hold fuses in electrical circuits. There are two main types: open fuse holders and fully enclosed fuse holders. The open fuse holders have exposed contact points and are commonly used in industries where space is limited or easy access to fuses is required. On the other hand, fully enclosed fuse holders provide a protective cover over the fuse, making them suitable for more demanding environments where safety and protection from dust, moisture, or accidental contact are crucial. Both types serve the purpose of securely holding fuses while facilitating their replacement or inspection.</p></p>
