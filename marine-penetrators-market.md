<p><p>Marine penetrators are vital components used in underwater applications to ensure reliable and watertight electrical and optical connections. There are three main types of marine penetrators in the market: electrical, optical, and hybrid. Electrical marine penetrators are designed to transmit electrical signals and power underwater. Optical marine penetrators enable the transmission of optical signals for communication and data transfer. Hybrid marine penetrators combine both electrical and optical capabilities, allowing for simultaneous transmission of power and data signals, hence providing a comprehensive solution for underwater connectivity requirements.</p></p>
