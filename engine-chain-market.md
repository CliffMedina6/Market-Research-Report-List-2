<p><p>The engine chain market encompasses two types: roller chain and silent chain. Roller chains consist of connected metal rollers that transmit mechanical power, widely used in motorcycles, bicycles, and industrial machinery. Conversely, silent chains operate with interconnected toothed plates, minimizing noise and vibration. They find applications in automobiles, household appliances, and industrial equipment. Both roller and silent chains play a crucial role in transmitting power between different components of engines, ensuring smooth operation and efficiency.</p></p>
