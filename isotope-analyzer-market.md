There are two main types of isotope analyzer markets: radioisotope analysis and stable isotope analysis. Radioisotope analysis involves the study of radioactive isotopes, which emit radiation and are used in various scientific applications such as medical imaging and radiocarbon dating. On the other hand, stable isotope analysis focuses on non-radioactive isotopes, providing insights into a wide range of fields such as environmental monitoring, nutrition studies, and geological research.</p></p>
