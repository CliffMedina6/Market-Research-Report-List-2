<p><p>Reflective sheeting is available in two main types: glass bead type and micro prismatic type. The glass bead type consists of tiny glass beads that reflect light to enhance visibility in low light conditions. It is widely used in road signs and traffic safety applications. On the other hand, the micro prismatic type is made of small prisms that reflect and refract light, providing a superior reflective effect. This type is commonly used in high-visibility clothing, vehicle markings, and other industrial safety applications. Both types greatly contribute to improving visibility and safety in various settings.</p></p>
