<p><p>A computer case, also known as a computer chassis, is an enclosure that holds and protects various components of a computer system. Computer cases come in different sizes and form factors to accommodate various types of motherboards and other components.</p><p>The computer case market has witnessed steady growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for customized PC builds, rising gaming and e-sports industry, and advancements in PC components and technologies. Manufacturers are introducing computer cases with built-in RGB lighting controllers, fan speed controllers, and connectivity ports for USB, audio, and video. This allows users to easily customize their PC builds and control various aspects of their systems.</p><p>Another trend is the focus on noise reduction and improved cooling performance. Computer case manufacturers are incorporating better fan designs, optimized airflow layouts, and sound-dampening materials to minimize noise and enhance cooling efficiency. These features are particularly appealing to gamers and professionals who require high-performance systems with minimal noise.</p><p>Overall, the computer case market is experiencing steady growth driven by the demand for customization, gaming, and advancements in PC components.
