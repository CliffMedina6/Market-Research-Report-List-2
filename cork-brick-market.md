<p><p>Cork bricks are categorized into two main types based on their market demand: round hole and non-round hole. The round hole market refers to the demand for cork bricks that have a circular hollow core running through them. These bricks are commonly used for insulation purposes in construction. On the other hand, the non-round hole market refers to the demand for cork bricks that do not have a circular hollow core. These bricks are typically used for decorative purposes or as a solid building material in various construction projects.</p></p>
