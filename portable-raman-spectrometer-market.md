The increasing demand for on-site chemical analysis in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and environmental monitoring is driving the growth of this market. <p><p>The Portable Raman Spectrometer market comprises different types based on the laser wavelength used for analysis. The 1064 nm type offers deeper penetration and is suitable for applications with complex sample matrices. The 785 nm type is commonly used for general purpose analysis with a balance between penetration depth and fluorescence interference. The 638 nm type offers lower fluorescence interference, making it suitable for analyzing samples with high fluorescence background. Lastly, the "Others" market encompasses spectrometers using different laser wavelengths, providing specific advantages for specialized applications.</p></p>
