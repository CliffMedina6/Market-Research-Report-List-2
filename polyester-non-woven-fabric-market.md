<p><strong>The Polyester Non-woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needle Punch</li><li>Stitch Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester non-woven fabric is widely used in various industries due to its excellent properties. Spunbond polyester non-woven fabric is made by extruding polyester filaments and bonding them together, providing durability and strength. Needle punch polyester non-woven fabric involves entangling polyester fibers by repeatedly puncturing them, resulting in increased thickness and density. Stitch bond polyester non-woven fabric is produced by mechanically interlocking polyester fibers through a stitching process, offering better dimensional stability and tear resistance. These different types cater to diverse applications, such as automotive, construction, hygiene, and filtration industries.</p></p>
