<p><p>A rotary arm stretch wrapper is a machine used in the packaging industry to secure pallet loads by wrapping them with stretch film. It consists of a rotating arm that moves around the stationary pallet, applying stretch film to hold the load in place during transportation or storage. This type of stretch wrapper is widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, logistics, and manufacturing.</p><p>The future outlook for the rotary arm stretch wrapper market is promising. The increasing adoption of automation in the packaging industry, along with the growing demand for efficient and secure pallet packaging solutions, is driving the market growth.
