<p><p>Spray foam sealant market consists of two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component spray foam sealants come in a ready-to-use form and do not require any additional mixing or preparation before application. They are simpler to use and suitable for small-scale sealing projects. On the other hand, two-component spray foam sealants require mixing of two different chemical components before use. This type offers higher performance, longer shelf life, and is commonly used for larger-scale construction and insulation projects.</p></p>
