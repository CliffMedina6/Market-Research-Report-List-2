<p><p>Jacketed vessels are containers used in various industries for heating or cooling purposes. There are different types of jackets available in the market. Conventional jackets are the most common type, consisting of a second outer vessel surrounding the main vessel. Half-pipe coil jackets are designed with a coil welded onto the vessel's exterior, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Dimple jackets have an intricate pattern of dimples on the outer vessel's surface, providing a large heat transfer area. Plate coil jackets consist of thin metal plates welded onto the outer vessel, enabling effective thermal conductivity.</p></p>
