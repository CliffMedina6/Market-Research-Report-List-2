<p><p>The MEMS Micromirrors market includes two main types of mirrros: mono-axial MEMS mirror and dual-axial MEMS mirror. A mono-axial MEMS mirror is a single-axis mirror that can rotate along one axis, offering precise control over the reflected light angle. It is commonly used in applications like laser scanning displays and optical switches. On the other hand, a dual-axial MEMS mirror can rotate along two axes, allowing for even more versatility in controlling the light beam. This type of mirror finds applications in areas such as bioimaging, spectroscopy, and 3D sensing.</p></p>
