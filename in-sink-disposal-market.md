<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The in-sink disposal market is divided into two main types: batch feed type and continuous feed type. Batch feed disposals require the user to load the waste into the disposal chamber and cover it before the device can be activated. They are considered safer but require more time and effort. On the other hand, continuous feed disposals allow waste to be added while the device is running, providing convenience but posing a higher risk. Both types cater to different consumer needs, offering varying levels of safety and convenience.</p></p>
