<p><p>The epoxy-based instant adhesive market includes two main types: cold cure adhesive and heat cure adhesive. Cold cure adhesive refers to adhesives that set and cure at room temperature, without the need for external heat. These adhesives are ideal for fastening small components and materials that are sensitive to heat. On the other hand, heat cure adhesive refers to adhesives that require external heat, typically through ovens or other heating equipment, to achieve curing and bonding. This type of adhesive is commonly used for larger applications or when greater bond strength is required.</p></p>
