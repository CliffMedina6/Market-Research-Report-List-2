<p><p>BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) direct thermal printing film is a specialty film used for on-demand printing applications. Unlike traditional thermal transfer printing, direct thermal printing does not require ribbons, and the printing process is achieved by selectively heating the film using a thermal print head. BOPP direct thermal printing film offers advantages like high durability, excellent print quality, and resistance to water, oil, and chemicals.</p><p>The BOPP direct thermal printing film market is witnessing significant growth due to its increasing adoption in various industries such as packaging, retail, healthcare, and logistics.
