<p><p>The Car VRLA Battery market primarily consists of two types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Battery and Gel Battery. AGM Battery utilizes a fiberglass separator to hold the electrolyte, allowing it to be tightly absorbed within the battery plates. This design enhances its ability to tolerate repeated deep discharges and offers a higher discharge rate. On the other hand, Gel Battery employs silica to turn the electrolyte into a gel-like substance, increasing its resistance to vibration and providing a longer lifespan. Both types have their unique advantages in the Car VRLA Battery market, catering to different customer needs.</p></p>
