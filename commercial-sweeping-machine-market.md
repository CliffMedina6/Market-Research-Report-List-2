<p><strong>The Commercial Sweeping Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ride-On Sweepers</li><li>Walk Behind/Pedestrian Sweepers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial sweeping machine market is divided into two types of machines: ride-on sweepers and walk-behind/pedestrian sweepers. Ride-on sweepers are larger machines that are suitable for larger areas and complex terrains. They offer higher efficiency and can cover more ground in less time compared to walk-behind sweepers. On the other hand, walk-behind/pedestrian sweepers are smaller and more maneuverable, making them ideal for tight or congested spaces. They are manually operated and require the user to walk behind the machine while sweeping. Both types serve different purposes and cater to different cleaning needs.</p></p>
