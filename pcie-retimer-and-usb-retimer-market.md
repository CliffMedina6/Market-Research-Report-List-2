<p><p>PCIe Retimers and USB Retimers are electronic devices used in computer systems to improve the performance and reliability of PCIe and USB connections, respectively.</p><p>PCIe Retimers are used in PCIe links to extend the reach and improve signal integrity. They allow longer distance transmission of PCIe signals without degradation, supporting higher data rates and enabling the use of thin and long cables. PCIe Retimers are commonly used in various applications, including data centers, servers, storage systems, and high-performance computing.</p><p>USB Retimers, on the other hand, are used in USB links to improve signal integrity and extend the transmission distance. They compensate for signal degradation caused by long cables, electromagnetic interference, or high-frequency noise, offering stable and reliable USB connections. USB Retimers are widely used in applications such as desktop and laptop computers, consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and automotive systems.</p><p>The growth of the PCIe Retimer and USB Retimer Market is driven by several factors.
