<p><p>The cellphone batteries market includes various types of batteries, such as nickel-cadmium (NiCd) batteries, nickel-hydrogen (NiH2) batteries, and lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Nickel-hydrogen batteries have higher energy density and longer lifespan than NiCd batteries, making them suitable for more advanced cellphones. However, lithium-ion batteries have gained dominance in the market due to their high energy density, longer lifespan, and lighter weight, making them the preferred choice for modern cellphones.</p></p>
