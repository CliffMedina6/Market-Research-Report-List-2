<p><p>The auto relay market consists of two main types: plug-in relays and PCB (printed circuit board) relays. Plug-in relays are versatile and can be easily inserted into a socket, making them convenient for replacement. They are commonly used in various automotive systems. On the other hand, PCB relays are directly mounted onto a printed circuit board, eliminating the need for additional wiring. They are generally smaller in size and offer cost-effective solutions for automobile applications. Both types of relays play a crucial role in ensuring efficient electrical operations in vehicles.</p></p>
