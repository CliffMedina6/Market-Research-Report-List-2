<p><strong>The Mask Review Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Review Machine market encompasses two main types: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the DD Method, the machine compares the masks used in the fabrication process to identify any differences between them. This is typically employed in the manufacturing of memory devices. On the other hand, the DB Method uses a database of existing patterns to verify the masks before manufacturing, ensuring their accuracy. This method is commonly used in the production of logic devices. Both methods contribute to enhancing quality control and efficiency in mask fabrication.</p></p>
