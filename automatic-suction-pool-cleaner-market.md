<p><p>The automatic suction pool cleaner market primarily consists of three types: robotic, suction-side, and pressure-side cleaners. Robotic cleaners are intelligent and independent, utilizing advanced technology to clean pools effectively. Suction-side cleaners rely on the pool's filtration system to create suction and collect debris. Pressure-side cleaners use the pressure from the pool's water return jets to move around and collect debris. Each type of cleaner has its advantages and suitability for specific pool types, sizes, and cleaning needs.</p></p>
