<p><p>Regenerative thermal oxidation (RTO) is a widely used method for air pollution control in industrial applications. It involves high-temperature combustion of harmful pollutants present in exhaust gases. RTO systems are available in different market types, namely rotary and compact. Rotary RTOs feature a rotating bed of ceramic material, allowing for excellent heat transfer and oxidation efficiency. Compact RTOs, on the other hand, have a smaller footprint and are suitable for installations with space limitations. Both types offer effective and energy-efficient solutions for pollution control in various industries.</p></p>
