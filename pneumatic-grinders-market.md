<p><p>The pneumatic grinders market can be divided into two types: pneumatic angle grinders and pneumatic straight grinders. Pneumatic angle grinders are handheld power tools used for grinding, cutting, and polishing applications. They are commonly used in metalworking and construction industries due to their versatility and ability to access tight spaces. On the other hand, pneumatic straight grinders are also handheld tools but are primarily used for grinding and deburring operations in metal fabrication. Both types of grinders are powered by compressed air and offer precise control and high durability.</p></p>
