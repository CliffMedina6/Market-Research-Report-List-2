<p><p>The carpet and rugs market offers various types of products, including woven, tufted, knotted, and needle-punched options. Woven carpets and rugs are created by interlacing yarns in a systematic manner, resulting in a durable and long-lasting product. Tufted carpets and rugs are produced by stitching yarns through a backing material, providing a softer texture. Knotted carpets and rugs involve hand-knotting individual threads, often resulting in intricate designs. Needle-punched carpets and rugs are made by mechanically interlocking fibers, offering a dense and resilient end product. Each market type offers unique characteristics to cater to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
