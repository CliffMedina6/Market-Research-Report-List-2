<p><p>Research stereo microscopes are widely used in scientific research and industries for magnifying and analyzing small objects or specimens. There are three main types of stereo microscopes: monocular stereoscopic microscopes, binocular stereoscopic microscopes, and trinocular stereoscopic microscopes. Monocular stereoscopic microscopes have a single eyepiece, making them more affordable and suitable for basic applications. Binocular stereoscopic microscopes have two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable viewing experience and better depth perception. Trinocular stereoscopic microscopes have an additional port to attach a camera, allowing for image or video capture during observation.</p></p>
