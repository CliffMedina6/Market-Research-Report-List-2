<p><p>The Sleeve Applicator market can be classified into two types: Linear Applicator and Rotary Applicator. Linear Applicators are used for applying sleeves onto containers in a straight line, making them suitable for high-speed production lines. On the other hand, Rotary Applicators employ a rotary motion to apply sleeves, allowing for a higher level of flexibility and precision. Both types of applicators are widely utilized in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics, offering efficient and reliable solutions for sleeve labeling needs.</p></p>
