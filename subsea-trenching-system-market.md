<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea trenching system market is classified into two main types: mechanical trenchers and jet trenchers. Mechanical trenchers involve the use of mechanical equipment to excavate the seabed and create trenches. On the other hand, jet trenchers utilize high-pressure water jets to excavate the seabed and create trenches. Both types of trenchers are used in offshore activities such as pipeline and cable laying, seabed intervention, and underwater construction. These systems play a vital role in ensuring safe and efficient operations in the subsea industry.</p></p>
