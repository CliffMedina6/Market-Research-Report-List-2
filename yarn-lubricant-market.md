<p><p>Yarn lubricant market consists of three main types - spin finish, coning oils, and knitting oils. Spin finish is used during the spinning process to improve the yarn's performance and reduce friction. Coning oils are used in the winding process to minimize yarn breakages and improve the cone formation. Knitting oils are applied during the knitting process to reduce friction and improve the overall efficiency. These lubricants play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and performance of the yarn, ensuring smooth production processes in the textile industry.</p></p>
