<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The P-channel MOSFET transistor market consists of two main types: Enhancement Type and Depletion Type. In Enhancement Type transistors, the channel is normally off, but when a positive voltage is applied between the gate and source terminals, it enhances the conductivity of the channel, allowing current to flow. On the other hand, Depletion Type transistors have a normally conducting channel, and applying a negative voltage between the gate and source terminals depletes the channel, reducing the conductivity and blocking current flow. These types cater to different requirements and applications in various industries.</p></p>
