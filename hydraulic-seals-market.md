<p><p>Hydraulic seals are important components used in hydraulic systems to prevent fluid leakage and contamination. There are three main types of hydraulic seals in the market: hydraulic piston seals, hydraulic rod seals, and hydraulic wiper seals. Hydraulic piston seals are used to seal the gap between the piston and cylinder bore, while hydraulic rod seals prevent fluid leakage along the rod. Hydraulic wiper seals, on the other hand, are used to keep out external contaminants and moisture. These seals play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of hydraulic systems.</p></p>
