<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Concrete (GFRC) is a composite material made up of a cementitious matrix embedded with randomly dispersed alkali-resistant glass fibers. It combines the benefits of glass fibers and concrete, resulting in a lightweight, durable, and versatile material. GFRC is widely used in the construction industry for architectural cladding, facades, countertops, and other decorative applications.</p><p>The global glass fiber reinforced concrete market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
