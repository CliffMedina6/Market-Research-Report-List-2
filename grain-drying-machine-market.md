<p><ul><li>Stationary Grain Drying Machine</li><li>Mobile Grain Drying Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grain drying machine market can be categorized into two types: stationary grain drying machine and mobile grain drying machine. Stationary grain drying machines are permanently installed on a particular site and are used for drying large quantities of grain. They are typically operated using electricity or gas and have a higher drying capacity. Mobile grain drying machines, on the other hand, are portable and can be moved to different locations as needed. They are smaller in size, have a lower drying capacity, and are often powered by diesel engines.</p></p>
