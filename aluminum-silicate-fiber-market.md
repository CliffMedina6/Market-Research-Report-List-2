<p><p>Aluminum silicate fiber, also known as ceramic fiber, is a type of refractory material that exhibits excellent thermal insulation properties. Aluminum silicate fiber is widely used in various industries, including petrochemical, iron & steel, aerospace, and automotive, due to its high-temperature resistance and low thermal conductivity.</p><p>The future outlook for the aluminum silicate fiber market appears promising.
