<p><p>Telecom testing equipment consists of various types such as general-purpose test equipment and mechanical test equipment. General-purpose test equipment is designed to measure and analyze different parameters within the telecom network, enabling testing and troubleshooting of various components and systems. This equipment includes oscilloscopes, signal generators, power meters, and spectrum analyzers. On the other hand, mechanical test equipment is used to assess the physical properties and durability of telecom devices, ensuring they can withstand environmental stress and comply with industry standards. Such equipment includes vibration testers, drop testers, and environmental chambers.</p></p>
