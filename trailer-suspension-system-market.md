<p><ul><li>Air Suspension Systems</li><li>Mechanical Suspension Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trailer suspension systems are used in trailers to provide support and cushioning to the load being carried. There are two main types of trailer suspension systems: air suspension systems and mechanical suspension systems. </p><p>Air suspension systems use air bags and compressors to adjust the suspension height and offer a smoother ride. They are commonly used in heavy-duty trailers and offer better load distribution and improved stability.</p><p>On the other hand, mechanical suspension systems rely on leaf springs or torsion bars to provide support. They are often used in lighter trailers and offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness. However, they may not provide as smooth a ride as air suspension systems.</p></p>
