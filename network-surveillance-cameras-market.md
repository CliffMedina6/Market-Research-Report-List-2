<p><strong>The Network Surveillance Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for network surveillance cameras can be categorized into two types: centralized IP cameras and decentralized IP cameras. Centralized IP cameras gather and process video data in a central location, usually a network video recorder, which offers better control and management. On the other hand, decentralized IP cameras operate independently and have built-in recording and processing capabilities, eliminating the need for a central server. Both types have distinct advantages, such as enhanced security and flexibility, and cater to different surveillance needs, ranging from small-scale to large-scale applications.</p></p>
