<p><p>Diffusion vacuum pumps are a type of high-performance vacuum pump mainly used in applications that require an extremely clean vacuum environment. They utilize the principle of molecular diffusion to create and maintain the required vacuum level.</p><p>These pumps consist of a heated oil vapor jet that generates a high-speed stream of vapor molecules. These molecules collide with gas molecules in the pump and transfer momentum to them, gradually increasing their speed and causing them to move towards the exhaust. This leads to the expulsion of gas molecules from the system, creating a low-pressure environment of high purity.</p><p>The diffusion vacuum pumps market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Diffusion Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Diffusion Pumps</li><li>Vapor Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diffusion vacuum pumps are used to create and maintain a vacuum by removing gas molecules from a specific area. Oil diffusion pumps operate by utilizing a jet of high-speed oil vapor to trap and pump gas molecules. They are widely used in various industries due to their high pumping speed and low ultimate pressure capabilities. On the other hand, vapor jet pumps rely on the momentum transfer between high-speed vapor jets and gas molecules to create a vacuum. They are commonly used in applications where oil contamination is a concern.</p></p>
