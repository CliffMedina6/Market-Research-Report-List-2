<p><p>ADAS camera lens market is classified into two types: CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras, or charge-coupled device cameras, are known for their high image quality and low noise levels, making them suitable for applications requiring precise image capture. On the other hand, CMOS cameras, or complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor cameras, offer advantages such as lower cost, lower power consumption, and faster readout speeds. These cameras are commonly used in ADAS systems, enabling advanced driver assistance features like lane departure warning, collision avoidance, and adaptive cruise control.</p></p>
