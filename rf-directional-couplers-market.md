<p><p>RF directional couplers are passive devices used in RF and microwave systems to split or combine power between two or more transmission lines. They allow power to be sampled from the main transmission line by a coupled port, while the forward and reverse power is isolated from each other at the coupled and output ports respectively. These devices are commonly used in applications such as power monitoring, signal sampling, and impedance matching.</p><p>The RF directional couplers market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for wireless communication systems, the expansion of 5G networks, and the growing adoption of RF power amplifiers in various industries are driving the market growth.
