<p><p>The aircraft accelerometer market can be divided into two types: Without Display Type and With Display Type. Without Display Type accelerometers are a basic form of measurement devices, detecting acceleration forces within an aircraft and transmitting the data to other instruments for analysis and display. On the other hand, With Display Type accelerometers not only measure acceleration but also have an integrated display screen that provides real-time data visualization. This enables pilots and engineers to directly observe and analyze the acceleration forces during flight operations.</p></p>
