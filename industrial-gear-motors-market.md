<p><p>Industrial gear motors refer to a type of machinery used in various industries for the purpose of driving mechanical systems. The market can be categorized into two main types: gearbox and gear motors. Gearboxes consist of multiple gears that transmit power, enabling speed and torque adjustments. They are commonly used in applications that require precision and high torque, such as conveyor systems and cranes. On the other hand, gear motors combine an electric motor with a gearbox, providing a compact and efficient solution for applications that require both power and speed control, like manufacturing machinery and automated systems.</p></p>
