<p><p>An automotive start-stop device is a system that automatically shuts off and restarts the internal combustion engine of a vehicle to reduce idling time and improve fuel efficiency. It works by cutting off the engine when the vehicle comes to a stop, such as at traffic lights, and then restarting it when the driver releases the brake pedal or engages the clutch.</p><p>The automotive start-stop device has gained significant popularity in recent years due to the increasing need for fuel efficiency and reducing vehicle emissions. It helps to save fuel by eliminating unnecessary idling time when the vehicle is not in motion. Moreover, it reduces emissions of greenhouse gases and pollutants, consequently contributing to a cleaner environment.</p><p>According to the market analysis, the automotive start-stop device market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.
