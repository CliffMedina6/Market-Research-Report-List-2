<p><ul><li>Analog A/V Cable</li><li>Digital A/V Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AV cables market consists of two main types: analog A/V cables and digital A/V cables. Analog A/V cables transmit audio and video signals in analog format, typically using composite, component, or S-video connections. These cables are widely used in older video equipment and provide satisfactory quality for standard definition signals. On the other hand, digital A/V cables transmit signals in digital format, such as HDMI or DVI, ensuring high-definition audio and video quality. They are commonly utilized in modern devices, including HDTVs, Blu-ray players, and gaming consoles, to deliver superior audiovisual experiences.</p></p>
