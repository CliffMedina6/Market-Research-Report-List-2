<p><p>The fire-resistant cable market comprises various types of cables used in different applications. XPLE cables offer excellent resistance to fire and high temperatures. LSZH cables, also known as low smoke zero halogen cables, emit limited smoke and no toxic halogen compounds when exposed to fire. PVC cables are made of polyvinyl chloride and provide good fire resistance. EPR cables are made of ethylene propylene rubber and offer high resistance to fire and extreme temperatures. Lastly, there are other types of fire-resistant cables available in the market that cater to specific needs and requirements of various industries.</p></p>
