<p><p>The Voltage Controlled Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (VCTCXO) market has three major types: Output PECL, Output CMOS, and Output SINEWAVE. Output PECL refers to a VCTCXO that produces a Positive Emitter-Coupled Logic (PECL) output signal, which is commonly used in high-speed data transmission. Output CMOS indicates a VCTCXO that yields a Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) output signal, suitable for various digital circuit applications. Output SINEWAVE signifies a VCTCXO that generates a sinusoidal output signal, commonly used in applications requiring a continuous wave output, such as telecommunications and RF systems.</p></p>
