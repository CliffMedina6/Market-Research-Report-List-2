Food wrap films provide a secure and sanitary way to package food, preventing exposure to environmental contaminants.</p><p>Additionally, the growing awareness about food safety and the importance of maintaining hygiene standards is fueling the demand for food wrap films. These films help seal in the freshness of the food and protect it from moisture, bacteria, and other harmful elements.
