<p><p>C4 LLDPE film is used in various industries for applications like packaging, agriculture, and construction. The market for C4 LLDPE film is categorized into three types: blown film extrusion, cast film extrusion, and others. Blown film extrusion involves a continuous process of melting and extruding the polymer through a circular die, creating a tube-like structure. Cast film extrusion involves the extrusion of the polymer through a flat die, resulting in a thin, flat sheet of film. The "others" category comprises niche processes like coextrusion and lamination, which are used for specific applications.</p></p>
