<p><strong>The Conductive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA).</li><li>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive adhesives are used for bonding electrical components in various industries. Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA) market involves adhesives that provide conductivity in all directions, making them suitable for applications where electrical connections need to be established between multiple components. On the other hand, Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA) market refers to adhesives that provide conductivity in a specific direction, typically used for connecting components with fine pitch or densely packed circuitry. Both types of conductive adhesives play a significant role in ensuring reliable electrical connections in electronics and other technological devices.</p></p>
