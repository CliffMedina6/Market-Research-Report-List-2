<p><p>Industrial scales are used in various industries to accurately measure the weight of objects. Different market types of industrial scales include bench scales, platform scales, floor scales, pallet truck scales, crane scales, and others. Bench scales are compact and typically used on workbenches or countertops. Platform scales have a larger weighing surface and are commonly used in warehouses or factories. Floor scales are heavy-duty scales designed to be placed directly on the floor. Pallet truck scales are integrated into pallet jacks for weighing loaded pallets. Crane scales are used to weigh heavy objects by attaching them to a crane. Other market types include specialized scales for specific industries or applications.</p></p>
