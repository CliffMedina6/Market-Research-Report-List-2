<p><p>Rubber vulcanization is a process that involves the addition of chemicals to natural or synthetic rubber to increase its strength, durability, and resistance to heat and aging. The rubber vulcanization market consists of three main types of chemicals: accelerators, vulcanizing agents, and activators. Accelerators speed up the vulcanization process, resulting in faster rubber curing. Vulcanizing agents promote the formation of cross-links between rubber molecules, increasing its strength and elasticity. Activators enhance the effectiveness of accelerators and vulcanizing agents, optimizing the vulcanization process for improved rubber performance.</p></p>
