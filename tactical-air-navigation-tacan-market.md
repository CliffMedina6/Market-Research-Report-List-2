<p><p>Tactical Air Navigation (TACAN) is a navigation system primarily used by military aircraft for air-to-air and air-to-ground navigation. It combines the functions of distance measuring equipment (DME) and VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) beacon, providing aircraft with accurate distance and bearing information. TACAN systems are widely adopted by defense forces around the world due to their effectiveness in enhancing situational awareness and improving navigation capabilities in both uncontrolled and hostile environments.</p><p>The Tactical Air Navigation (TACAN) Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for advanced navigation systems by defense forces to enhance their operational efficiency is a major driving factor for the market growth. These systems are being developed with improved accuracy, longer ranges, and enhanced electronic counter-countermeasures capabilities to meet the evolving requirements of modern military operations.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a shift towards implementing TACAN systems with integrated GPS capabilities, enabling more accurate and reliable navigation in GPS-denied environments.
