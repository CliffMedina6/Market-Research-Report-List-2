<p><p>Yacht windows are available in two main types: openable yacht windows and fixed yacht windows. Openable yacht windows are designed to be opened, allowing for ventilation and fresh air inside the yacht. They offer flexibility and can be adjusted to control the amount of air circulation. On the other hand, fixed yacht windows cannot be opened and are designed to provide a clear view while ensuring the structural integrity of the yacht. They are typically made with non-opening glass panels and are more secure against water leaks and external elements.</p></p>
