<p><p>Parafilm is a protective film commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings. Thermoplastic parafilm refers to a type of parafilm that can be softened and re-molded when heated, making it flexible and easily conformable. It is widely used in applications where a tight seal and stretchability are required. Conversely, non-thermoplastic parafilm does not soften when heated and is typically used for applications where durability and stability are more important than flexibility. Both types cater to different market needs and offer unique benefits based on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
