<p><p>Residential lighting fixtures market encompasses various types such as incandescent, fluorescent, LED, and OLED. Incandescent lighting fixtures use a filament inside a bulb, emitting light when heated electrically; however, they are less energy-efficient compared to other options. Fluorescent fixtures employ a gas-filled tube to produce light when electricity excites the gases. LED fixtures utilize light-emitting diodes to emit bright and energy-efficient light. OLED fixtures incorporate organic compounds to generate light, enabling flexible and thin lighting solutions. These different types cater to the diverse lighting needs of residential spaces, offering varying levels of energy efficiency and design flexibility.</p></p>
