<p><p>GPS (Global Positioning System) and GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) receivers are devices that use satellite signals to determine the precise location, velocity, and time synchronization of an object or person. These receivers collect signals from multiple satellites in the GPS and GNSS constellations and use triangulation techniques to calculate the position accurately.</p><p>The GPS & GNSS Receivers Market is expected to see significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for accurate positioning and navigation systems across various industries, including aerospace and defense, transportation and logistics, and agriculture, is driving market growth. Moreover, the rising use of smartphones and wearable devices with integrated GPS receivers is also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>One of the major trends in the GPS & GNSS receivers market is the growing adoption of real-time kinematics (RTK) technology. RTK receivers provide highly accurate positioning by using carrier phase measurements and correction data from a reference station. This technology finds extensive applications in surveying, precision agriculture, and construction.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of GPS and GNSS receivers with other sensors and technologies such as cameras, LiDAR, and inertial measurement units (IMUs). This integration enables enhanced navigation and mapping capabilities for applications like autonomous vehicles, drones, and augmented reality.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in receiver technologies, such as the development of multi-constellation and multi-frequency receivers, are expected to propel market growth. These receivers can track signals from multiple satellite constellations, including GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, resulting in improved accuracy and reliability.</p><p>In conclusion, the GPS & GNSS receivers market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for accurate positioning and navigation solutions. The adoption of RTK technology, integration with other sensors, and advancements in receiver technologies are some of the key trends driving market expansion. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period.</p></p>
