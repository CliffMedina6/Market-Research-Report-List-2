<p><p>The laser film market comprises three main types: PET laser film, BOPP laser film, and PVC laser film. PET laser film is made from polyethylene terephthalate and offers excellent dimensional stability and transparency, making it suitable for packaging, printing, and lamination purposes. BOPP laser film, made from biaxially oriented polypropylene, provides high resistance to heat, moisture, and chemicals, making it ideal for labels and flexible packaging. PVC laser film, made from polyvinyl chloride, offers good flexibility, transparency, and printability, making it suitable for various applications, including stickers and decorative materials.</p></p>
