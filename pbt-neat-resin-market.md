It offers excellent dimensional stability, high heat resistance, and good electrical properties. On the other hand, Extrusion Grade PBT is used for extrusion processes, where the resin is melted and forced through a die to create products like sheets, films, and fibers. It possesses similar properties as Injection Grade PBT but is formulated for extrusion applications.</p></p>
