<p><p>The Rail-Mount Temperature Transmitters market includes three main types of temperature sensors: Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), Thermocouples, and Resistance-Type Remote Sensors. RTDs measure temperature by correlating resistance changes in a metal wire as temperature changes. Thermocouples generate voltage signals based on temperature differences at junctions of different metal types. Resistance-Type Remote Sensors work by sensing temperature based on changes in electrical resistance. All three types of temperature sensors are commonly used in industrial applications where precise and accurate temperature measurement and monitoring are required.</p></p>
