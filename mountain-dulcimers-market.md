<p><p>Mountain dulcimers come in two main market types: all solid wood and laminated wood. All solid wood mountain dulcimers are constructed entirely from solid pieces of wood, such as mahogany or spruce, resulting in a richer tone and greater resonance. These instruments are often preferred by professional players and enthusiasts who value high-quality sound. On the other hand, laminated wood mountain dulcimers are made by layering thin sheets of wood together, resulting in a more affordable option. While they may not produce the same level of sound quality as all solid wood dulcimers, they still offer a pleasant and enjoyable playing experience for beginners and casual players.</p></p>
