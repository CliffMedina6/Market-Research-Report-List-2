<p><p>A flap disc is a grinding and polishing tool that consists of a flat circular shape with overlapping abrasive flaps glued onto it. These flaps are made of various abrasive materials such as aluminum oxide, zirconia alumina, ceramic, or silicon carbide. The market is driven by the increasing demand for high-quality and cost-effective abrasive tools in various industries.
