<p><strong>The Fixed RFID Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed RFID systems refer to a type of radio frequency identification technology used in various industries for tracking and identification purposes. LF (Low-Frequency) RFID systems operate at 125-134 kHz and are commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF (High-Frequency) RFID systems operate at 13.56 MHz and can be found in applications like payment systems and library book tracking. UHF (Ultra-High-Frequency) RFID systems operate at 860-960 MHz and are used in inventory management and supply chain operations. "Others" category includes systems operating at different frequencies or hybrid solutions tailored to specific requirements.</p></p>
