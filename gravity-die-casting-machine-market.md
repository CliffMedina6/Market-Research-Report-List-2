<p><p>Gravity die casting machines are used in the metal casting industry to produce high-quality castings. There are two main types of machines: the die horizontal opening machines and the die vertical opening machines. Die horizontal opening machines have a horizontal parting line, where the two halves of the die move horizontally to open and close the mold. On the other hand, die vertical opening machines have a vertical parting line, where the two halves of the die move vertically. Both machines offer different advantages depending on the specific application and desired casting outcome.</p></p>
