<p><p>A light duty hydraulic hammer, also known as a hydraulic breaker, is a construction equipment tool used for breaking or demolishing concrete, rocks, and other hard materials. The light duty hydraulic hammer is designed for smaller-scale construction projects and is typically more portable and lighter than its heavy-duty counterparts.</p><p>The light duty hydraulic hammer market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the coming years.
