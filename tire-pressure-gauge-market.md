<p><p>The tire pressure gauge market is comprised of two types: dial type and digital type. Dial type tire pressure gauges feature a physical dial that shows the pressure reading, with a needle pointing to the corresponding measurement. On the other hand, digital type tire pressure gauges display the reading on a digital screen. The dial type gauges are more traditional and often less expensive, while digital ones are generally more accurate and may offer additional features like backlighting or auto-shutoff. Both types serve the same purpose of measuring tire pressure accurately.</p></p>
