<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Laser Marking Head</li><li>CO2 Laser Marking Head</li><li>Fiber Laser Marking Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser marking heads market can be classified into three types: semiconductor laser marking head, CO2 laser marking head, and fiber laser marking head. Semiconductor laser marking heads use a solid-state laser to create marks on different materials. CO2 laser marking heads employ a gas laser that emits infrared light for precise marking. Fiber laser marking heads use a fiber laser source that generates laser beams through a fiber optic cable for accurate and high-speed marking. These different types cater to various industrial applications with their unique characteristics and performance capabilities.</p></p>
