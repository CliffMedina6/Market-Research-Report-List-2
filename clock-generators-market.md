<p><ul><li>Generation Only</li><li>Distribution and Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clock generators are electronic devices used in various applications to produce clock signals that synchronize and control the timing of digital circuits. In the market, clock generators are categorized into three types: generation only, distribution, and generation. </p><p>Generation-only clock generators are designed solely to generate clock signals, offering precise and stable timing for a single circuit or system. Distribution clock generators distribute a single clock signal to multiple circuits or systems, ensuring synchronization and reducing the need for multiple individual clock generators. Generation and distribution clock generators offer both functions, generating clock signals and distributing them to multiple circuits or systems simultaneously, providing precise timing and synchronization across multiple devices.</p></p>
