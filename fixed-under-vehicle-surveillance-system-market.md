<p><p>A Fixed Under Vehicle Surveillance System (FUVSS) is a security technology used to inspect the undercarriage of a vehicle for any hidden or suspicious objects such as explosives, drugs, or weapons. This system typically consists of fixed cameras and smart imaging software that capture and analyze the undercarriage images in real-time. FUVSS is widely used in high-security areas such as airports, border crossings, government buildings, and military bases.</p><p>The market analysis of the Fixed Under Vehicle Surveillance System Market indicates a positive growth trajectory. The increasing concerns over global security threats, terrorism, and illicit activities have driven the demand for advanced security systems like FUVSS. These technologies enable real-time monitoring and remote access to FUVSS, offering improved convenience and operational efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in camera technology, such as higher resolution and increased field of view, are contributing to the adoption of FUVSS.
