<p><p>A helium leak detector is a device used to detect and locate leaks in systems or containers that are under vacuum or pressure. It works by drawing a vacuum on the system and sensing the presence of helium, which is an ideal tracer gas due to its small atomic size, inertness, and low background level in the environment.</p><p>The helium leak detector market has witnessed steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 2.80% during the forecast period. One of the major drivers of market growth is the increasing demand for helium leak detectors in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and pharmaceutical, where the detection of leaks is crucial for safety and quality control.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have improved the sensitivity and accuracy of helium leak detectors, making them more reliable and efficient. For instance, the introduction of mass spectrometer-based leak detectors has enhanced the detection capabilities, allowing for the detection of leaks as small as 1x10-12 atm cc/s.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing emphasis on environmental protection and sustainability has also contributed to market growth. Helium leak detectors are used for detecting leaks in refrigeration and air conditioning systems, preventing the release of harmful greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards the adoption of portable and handheld helium leak detectors.
