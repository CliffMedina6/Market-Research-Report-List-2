<p><strong>The Electric Sanders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Type</li><li>Disc Type</li><li>Orbital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric sanders are power tools used for smoothing or shaping surfaces. Belt-type sanders consist of a continuous sanding belt, making them suitable for large, flat areas. Disc-type sanders have a rotating circular sanding disc perfect for precise sanding and finishing. Orbital-type sanders have a vibrating or rotating sanding pad, providing a random or circular pattern making them versatile for various tasks. Belt-type sanders are commonly used in woodworking, disc-type sanders are favored for metal polishing, while orbital-type sanders are ideal for general purpose sanding and preparing surfaces for paint or varnish.</p></p>
