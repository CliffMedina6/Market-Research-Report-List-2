<p><p>The damper spring module market comprises two main types: conventional dampers and multi-adjustment dampers. Conventional dampers are the traditional type and provide a standard level of damping force. They are designed to absorb and dissipate shocks and vibrations. On the other hand, multi-adjustment dampers offer the ability to customize the damping force according to specific needs. They provide different settings that can be adjusted to optimize performance, comfort, and handling. Both types serve to enhance vehicle stability and control, with multi-adjustment dampers offering greater flexibility in fine-tuning the suspension system.</p></p>
