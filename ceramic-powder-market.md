Nitride ceramic powder includes compounds like aluminum nitride and silicon nitride, utilized for their thermal conductivity in electronic devices. Oxide ceramic powder consists of materials like alumina and zirconia, employed in the production of ceramics and refractories. Other ceramic powders are specific varieties tailored for various applications in construction, automotive, and healthcare sectors.</p></p>
