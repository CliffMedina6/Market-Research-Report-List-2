<p><p>The industrial ultrafast lasers market encompasses various types of lasers used for industrial purposes. Titanium-sapphire lasers are known for their broad tunability and allow for precise control over pulse duration. Diode-pumped lasers utilize semiconductor diode technology for efficient energy conversion. Fiber lasers utilize fiber optics for laser amplification, resulting in high-power and compact systems. Mode-locked diode lasers provide ultrafast pulses using a diode laser as the gain medium. Other types of ultrafast lasers include solid-state lasers and semiconductor lasers, each with their own advantages in industrial applications.</p></p>
