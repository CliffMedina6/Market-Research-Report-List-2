<p><p>The ion exchange chromatography system market consists of two types: single channel and multi-channel systems. Single channel systems can process one sample at a time, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for laboratories with lower sample volumes. On the other hand, multi-channel systems can handle multiple samples simultaneously, providing higher throughput and efficiency for laboratories with larger sample volumes. Both types are widely utilized in the field of chromatography to separate and analyze ions based on their affinity towards charged stationary phases within the system.</p></p>
