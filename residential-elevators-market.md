<p><p>Residential elevators market types include platform lifts, cabin lifts, and other lifts. Platform lifts are designed for easy wheelchair access and are often installed outdoors. Cabin lifts are enclosed elevators that provide vertical transportation inside homes. They are typically installed in multilevel residences and offer a smoother and more luxurious ride. Other lifts refer to a variety of elevator types, such as tube lifts or vacuum elevators, which are less common but are gaining popularity due to their unique designs and space-saving features. These elevator options cater to different residential needs and preferences.</p></p>
