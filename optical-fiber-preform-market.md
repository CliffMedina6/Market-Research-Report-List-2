<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Preform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VAD</li><li>OVD</li><li>PCVD</li><li>MCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber preform market consists of four main types: VAD (Vapor Axial Deposition), OVD (Outside Vapor Deposition), PCVD (Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition), and MCVD (Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition). VAD involves depositing multiple layers of materials inside a rotating tube to form the preform. OVD utilizes a burner to deposit a soot layer on the outside of a rotating substrate tube. PCVD involves passing reactive gases through a plasma to create a soot layer on the inside of the tube. MCVD relies on the reaction between gases and the substrate tube surface to form the soot layer.</p></p>
