<p><p>LED stair lighting market consists of two types - LED Step Light and LED Strip Light. LED Step Light is designed specifically to be installed on each individual step of the staircase, providing sufficient lighting and enhancing safety. On the other hand, LED Strip Light is a flexible strip with adhesive backing that can be mounted along the staircase to create an ambient lighting effect. Both types offer energy-efficient and long-lasting lighting solutions for stairs, catering to different preferences and styles.</p></p>
