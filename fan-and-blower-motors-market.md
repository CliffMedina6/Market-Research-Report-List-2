<p><p>The Fan and Blower Motors Market is primarily categorized into three types: centrifugal motor, axial flow motor, and others. Centrifugal motors are designed to move air or gas in a radial direction outwards from the center, making them ideal for applications requiring high pressure and low airflow. Axial flow motors, on the other hand, move air or gas parallel to the axis of rotation, generating a large volume of airflow at relatively lower pressures. The "others" category encompasses motors that possess unique characteristics or combinations of both centrifugal and axial flow motor features.</p></p>
