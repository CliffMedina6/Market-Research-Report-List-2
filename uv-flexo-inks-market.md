<p><p>The UV flexo inks market is divided into different types based on their composition and characteristics. These inks are suitable for applications that require good adhesion and fast drying times. On the other hand, solvent-based UV flexo inks use organic solvents as the main component, offering excellent color and durability. These inks are commonly used in applications that demand high print quality and resistance to harsh environments.</p></p>
