<p><p>Traffic lights are a signaling device used to control the flow of vehicle and pedestrian traffic at road intersections. They typically consist of three colored lights, red, yellow, and green, mounted vertically on a pole. Traffic lights play a crucial role in ensuring road safety and smooth traffic flow, reducing the risk of accidents and congestion.</p><p>The Traffic Lights Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
