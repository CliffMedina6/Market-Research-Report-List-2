<p><p>Antimicrobial plastics are divided into three main market types: commodity plastic, engineering plastic, and high performance plastic. Commodity plastics, such as polyethylene and polypropylene, are widely used in everyday products due to their low cost and easy manufacturing process. Engineering plastics, like polycarbonate and polyamide, possess better mechanical and thermal properties, making them suitable for more demanding applications. High-performance plastics, such as polyetheretherketone and polyphenylene sulfide, exhibit exceptional properties like enhanced temperature resistance and chemical inertness, making them ideal for specialized industries like healthcare and aerospace.</p></p>
