<p><p>The Household Ventilation Fan market includes three main types: Ceiling Fans, Wall-Mounted Fans, and Window-Mounted Fans. Ceiling Fans are mounted on the ceiling and circulate air downward. They are commonly used in large rooms or outdoor spaces. Wall-Mounted Fans are affixed to a wall and provide a localized breeze. They are suitable for smaller rooms or areas with limited space. Window-Mounted Fans are designed to fit into windows and promote air circulation by drawing in fresh air from outside. They are often used in kitchens, bathrooms, or small rooms.</p></p>
