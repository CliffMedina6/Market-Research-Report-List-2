<p><p>The automotive rear-view camera systems market includes two types: wired rear-view camera systems and wireless rear-view camera systems. Wired rear-view camera systems require a physical connection between the camera and the display unit, using cables. This type offers a reliable and stable video transmission, but installation can be more complex. On the other hand, wireless rear-view camera systems transmit video signals without cables, using wireless technology. This type provides easier installation, but the video transmission may be less stable and prone to interference. Both types aim to improve safety by providing a clear view of the rear surroundings while driving.</p></p>
