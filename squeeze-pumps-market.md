<p><p>Squeeze pumps, also known as peristaltic pumps or hose pumps, are positive displacement pumps that use rotating rollers to squeeze fluid through a flexible tube or hose. These pumps are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceutical, food and beverage, water treatment, and chemical, among others. They are preferred in applications where a gentle pumping action is required, as they do not produce any shear or damage to the fluid being pumped.</p><p>The current outlook of the squeeze pumps market is highly positive.
