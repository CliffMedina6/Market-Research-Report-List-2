<p><p>Mechanical Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) Equipment is used for rapidly freezing food products individually, allowing them to retain their shape, texture, and taste. There are two types of IQF equipment: Spiral IQF and Tunnel IQF. </p><p>Spiral IQF equipment utilizes a continuous spiral conveyor belt system, which ensures a uniform freezing process. It is suitable for smaller capacities and requires less floor space.</p><p>Tunnel IQF equipment consists of a series of freezing chambers, wherein products are placed on trays or conveyed on belts. It is ideal for larger capacities and offers higher production rates.</p></p>
