<p><strong>The Patient Blood Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surgical Stage</li><li>Non-Surgical Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patient Blood Management (PBM) involves various stages to optimize the patient's blood supply while minimizing blood loss and preventing unnecessary blood transfusions. The Surgical Stage of PBM focuses on interventions before, during, and after a surgical procedure to reduce potential blood loss, such as minimizing incisions and using advanced hemostatic techniques. The Non-Surgical Stage of PBM addresses strategies for managing anemia, optimizing blood volume, and limiting blood loss in non-surgical patients, like those with chronic conditions or undergoing medical treatments. Both stages aim to improve patient outcomes, reduce complications, and enhance blood conservation practices.</p></p>
