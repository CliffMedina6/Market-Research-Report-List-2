<p><p>The Power Liftgate Motor Market encompasses two main types: DC Brushed Motor and DC Brushless Motor. DC Brushed Motors use brushes and a commutator to transmit electrical current, converting it into mechanical motion. While they are cost-effective and provide good torque, they are prone to wear and require regular maintenance. On the other hand, DC Brushless Motors employ electronic commutation and offer improved performance, higher efficiency, and longer lifespan. They are commonly used in applications that demand high reliability and stability.</p></p>
