<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The sintered diamond saw blades market consists of two major types: hot pressed sintered and cold press sintered blades. Hot pressed sintered blades are manufactured by heating the diamond powder and metal matrix simultaneously, resulting in higher blade density and strength. These blades are suitable for cutting hard materials such as granite, concrete, and engineered stones. On the other hand, cold press sintered blades are made by mixing diamond powder and metal matrix and then pressing them together at room temperature. These blades are more cost-effective and ideal for cutting softer materials like asphalt and green concrete.</p></p>
