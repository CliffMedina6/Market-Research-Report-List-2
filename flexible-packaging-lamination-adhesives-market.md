<p><p>Flexible Packaging Lamination Adhesives are used in the packaging industry to bind layers of flexible packaging materials together. There are three types: Solvent Based Adhesives, which contain solvents for bonding; Solvent-free Adhesives, which do not contain solvents and are eco-friendly; and Water Based Adhesives, which use water as a carrier for bonding. Solvent Based Adhesives offer strong bond strength but have environmental concerns. Solvent-free Adhesives are environmentally friendly and have good adhesion properties. Water Based Adhesives are also eco-friendly and provide excellent bond strength, making them popular in the market.</p></p>
