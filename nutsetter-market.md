<p><ul><li>Hex Nutsetter</li><li>Lobular Nutsetter</li><li>Impact Nutsetter</li><li>Torsion Nutsetter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nutsetters are tools used to tighten or loosen nuts. There are various market types of nutsetters available. Hex nutsetters are commonly used and have a six-sided shape for a strong grip on hexagonal nuts. Lobular nutsetters have rounded lobes that exert force on the nut's flat sides, minimizing slipping. Impact nutsetters are designed for heavy-duty applications and can withstand high torque levels. Torsion nutsetters have flexible bodies that absorb torque, reducing stress and preventing damage to the nut or bolt. Each market type serves different purposes based on the requirements of the task at hand.</p></p>
