<p><p>Aviation infrastructure refers to the physical facilities, systems, and services necessary for the operation and management of airports, air traffic control, and other aviation-related activities. The increasing air travel demand and government initiatives to enhance airport infrastructure have been the key drivers of this growth. The market is also being driven by the need for airports to handle larger aircraft, accommodate more passengers, and improve overall operational efficiency.</p><p>Additionally, the growing focus on modernization and capacity expansion of existing airports to meet the growing air travel demand is fueling market growth. With the advent of low-cost carriers and the expansion of international tourism, airports are facing the challenge of accommodating larger volumes of passengers and aircraft. As a result, there is a growing demand for infrastructure upgrades and new airport construction projects.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several key trends that are shaping its growth. There is a growing emphasis on the use of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, biometrics, and IoT in aviation infrastructure to enhance safety, security, and operational efficiency.
