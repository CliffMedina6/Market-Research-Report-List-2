<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Galvanized Reinforcing Bar Market comprises two main types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar undergoes a traditional galvanizing process where the rebars are dipped in molten zinc to form a protective coating. Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar involves a continuous galvanizing process, where the rebars pass through a series of zinc baths to create the protective coating. Both types aim to enhance the durability and corrosion resistance of the reinforcing bar, ensuring their longevity in various construction applications.</p></p>
