<p><p>Laser technology is classified into three main types: solid laser, liquid laser, and gas laser. Solid lasers utilize solid materials, such as crystals or glasses, as the active medium to generate laser beams. Liquid lasers employ liquids, such as dye solutions, to produce laser radiation. Gas lasers use gases, including helium-neon or carbon dioxide, to create laser beams. Each type of laser technology has its own unique properties and applications, making them suitable for various fields such as medicine, communications, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
