<p><p>The Side Load Case Packer market can be categorized into two types: Continuous Case Packer and Intermittent Case Packer. Continuous Case Packers are designed for high-speed packaging lines, where products are constantly fed into the machine without stopping the process. These packers are ideal for industries that require continuous production and use automated feeding systems. On the other hand, Intermittent Case Packers are suitable for lower-speed packaging lines that require periodic stopping and starting of the packaging process, making them more flexible for industries with diverse product lines or smaller production volumes.</p></p>
