<p><p>Ethernet Storage Fabric (ESF) is a high-speed, low-latency network infrastructure that enables the transfer and storage of data between servers and storage systems. One of the prominent trends is the increasing adoption of software-defined storage (SDS) solutions, which provide greater flexibility, scalability, and cost savings. The integration of Ethernet Storage Fabrics with SDS solutions is gaining significant traction, as it enables organizations to optimize storage resources and simplify management.</p><p>Another trend is the migration from traditional Fibre Channel (FC) networks to Ethernet-based storage networks.
