<p><p>The stationary barcode scanner market comprises three main types: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imager scanners. Laser scanners use a laser beam to scan and capture the barcode information. Linear imagers, on the other hand, use an image sensor to capture the barcode data. Finally, 2D imager scanners are capable of capturing not only linear barcodes but also more complex 2D barcodes using an array of sensors. These different types cater to various industries and scanning requirements, providing versatility and efficiency in barcode scanning applications.</p></p>
