<p><strong>The Scientific Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laboratory Analytical Instruments and Consumables(LAIC)</li><li>Measuring and Monitoring Devices(MMD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for scientific instruments can be categorized into two types - Laboratory Analytical Instruments and Consumables (LAIC) and Measuring and Monitoring Devices (MMD). LAIC includes instruments used in laboratories for analysis purposes, as well as the consumables required for them. This includes items like spectrometers, chromatographs, and reagents. On the other hand, MMD refers to instruments used for measuring and monitoring various parameters like temperature, pressure, and humidity. This includes devices such as thermometers, pressure gauges, and humidity sensors. Both LAIC and MMD play crucial roles in scientific research and experimentation.</p></p>
