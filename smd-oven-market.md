<p><strong>The SMD Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Oven</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMD (Surface Mount Device) oven market consists of various types of ovens used in the electronic manufacturing industry. Convection ovens utilize hot air circulation to evenly distribute heat, ensuring efficient soldering of SMD components. Vapour phase ovens utilize a liquid or vaporized medium with a lower boiling point than the solder to create a controlled atmosphere for soldering. Both types of ovens provide precise temperature control, allowing for the soldering of SMD components effectively and efficiently.</p></p>
