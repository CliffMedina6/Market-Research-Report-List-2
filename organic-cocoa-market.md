<p><p>The organic cocoa market consists of three main segments: cocoa liquor, cocoa powder, and cocoa butter. Cocoa liquor is produced by grinding cocoa nibs into a liquid form, which serves as a base for chocolate products. Cocoa powder is obtained by removing cocoa butter from the cocoa liquor, resulting in a dry and highly concentrated powder used for baking, beverages, and confectionery. Finally, cocoa butter is the fat extracted from the cocoa bean and is commonly used in the production of various cosmetic and pharmaceutical products due to its moisturizing and healing properties.</p></p>
