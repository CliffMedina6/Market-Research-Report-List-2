<p><p>Polyester Fiber market comprises two main types: Polyester Staple Fiber (PSF) and Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY). PSF refers to a short-length synthetic fiber made from polyester, widely used in various applications such as textiles, furnishings, and non-woven materials. PFY, on the other hand, refers to a long and continuous polyester fiber that is mainly utilized in the production of fabrics for apparel and home textiles. Both PSF and PFY play crucial roles in the textile industry, offering durability, affordability, and versatility to meet diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
