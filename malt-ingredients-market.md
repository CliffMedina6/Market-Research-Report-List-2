<p><p>The malt ingredients market comprises different types of products, including dry extracts, liquid extracts, and malt flour. Dry extracts are made by evaporating the liquid content from malted grains, resulting in a concentrated powder that can be used to enhance flavor and color in various food and beverage applications. Liquid extracts, on the other hand, are obtained by dissolving malted grains in water, creating a syrup-like solution that adds sweetness and flavor to products. Lastly, malt flour is created by grinding malted grains into a fine powder, commonly used as a baking ingredient.</p></p>
