<p><p>The Flexo Plate Mounter market is classified into two main types: Semi-automatic and Fully Automatic. It involves positioning the plate on the mounter manually and using various controls to align and secure it in place. On the other hand, in the fully automatic market, the plate mounting process is completely automated. The mounter automatically positions and aligns the plate, using advanced sensors and computer-controlled systems, thereby reducing the need for manual intervention and improving efficiency.</p></p>
