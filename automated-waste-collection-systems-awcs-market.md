<p><p>Automated Waste Collection Systems (AWCS) is an efficient method of waste collection that utilizes pneumatic systems to transport waste from various collection points to a central disposal area. This market can be categorized into two types: gravity system and full vacuum system. Gravity systems rely on the force of gravity to move waste through underground pipes, making it suitable for downhill areas. On the other hand, full vacuum systems use suction to transport waste, suitable for both uphill and downhill areas. Both systems offer improved waste management and reduced manual labor.</p></p>
