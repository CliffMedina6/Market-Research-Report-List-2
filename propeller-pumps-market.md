<p><p>Propeller pumps are devices used to move large volumes of fluid with the help of rotating propeller blades. Vertical propeller pumps are designed to be installed vertically, with the motor located above the fluid level. They are commonly used in applications such as sewage treatment. Horizontal propeller pumps, on the other hand, are installed horizontally and are used in applications such as irrigation and flood control. Both types of propeller pumps are efficient in moving large volumes of fluid but differ in their installation and application.</p></p>
