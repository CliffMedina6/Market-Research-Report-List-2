<p><p>Metal heat treatment involves the application of heat to alter the properties of metal in order to enhance its mechanical properties and increase its durability. There are two main types of metal heat treatment markets: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment refers to the overall heating process of the entire metal part to change its internal structure. Surface heat treatment, on the other hand, focuses on modifying the properties of the metal's surface layer only, aiming to improve wear resistance, hardness, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
