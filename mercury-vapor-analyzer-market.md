<p><p>The Mercury Vapor Analyzer market consists of two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption and Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence. In Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption, the analyzer measures the mercury concentration in a sample by measuring the absorption of light. On the other hand, in Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence, the analyzer uses a different approach by measuring the fluorescence emitted by mercury atoms. Both methods are commonly used for environmental monitoring and industrial applications to detect and quantify mercury levels accurately.</p></p>
