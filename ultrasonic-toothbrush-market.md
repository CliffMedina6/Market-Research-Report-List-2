<p><p>In the Ultrasonic Toothbrush market, there are two main types: Single Frequency and Dual Frequency toothbrushes. Single Frequency toothbrushes operate at a fixed frequency, generally between 20,000 to 30,000 vibrations per minute, to provide effective cleaning. On the other hand, Dual Frequency toothbrushes offer the capability to switch between two frequencies, such as 20,000 and 40,000 vibrations per minute. This dual-mode allows for customized brushing experience, enabling users to select the frequency that suits their preferences or specific dental needs.</p></p>
