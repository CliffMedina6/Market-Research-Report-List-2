Acrylic coatings provide good moisture resistance and UV stability. Urethane coatings offer chemical resistance and durability. Epoxy coatings provide high mechanical strength and chemical resistance. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of coatings with unique properties and applications, such as fluoropolymer coatings or nanocoatings.</p></p>
