<p><p>The harbour crane market consists of two main types: mobile and fixed cranes. Mobile cranes are equipped with wheels or tracks, allowing them to be easily moved and repositioned within the harbor as needed. Their flexibility and mobility make them ideal for handling cargo in various locations. On the other hand, fixed cranes are permanently installed in one specific location within the port. They are mainly used for continuous operations in a dedicated area, such as loading and unloading containers from ships.</p></p>
