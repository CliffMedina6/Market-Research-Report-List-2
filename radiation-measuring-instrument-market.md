Scintillation detectors utilize a scintillator material that emits light upon interaction with radiation. Neutron detectors are designed specifically to measure neutron radiation. Geiger counters detect and count ionizing radiation by amplifying electrical impulses generated when radiation interacts with gas in a chamber. Other instruments encompass a wide range of devices, including dosimeters, spectrometers, and survey meters, offering diverse capabilities to measure and monitor different types of radiation.</p></p>
