<p><p>Current monitoring relays are electronic devices used to detect and monitor current levels in electrical systems. They are classified into two types: under-current detection and over-current detection. </p><p>Under-current detection relays are designed to sense current levels below a specific set value. They protect electrical equipment from malfunctions caused by low current, such as motor stalling or overheating. </p><p>On the other hand, over-current detection relays monitor current levels above a predetermined threshold. They provide protection against issues like short circuits and overloads, preventing damage to the electrical system and minimizing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
