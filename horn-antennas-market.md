<p><p>Horn antennas come in different types, including Twistable Waveguide, Seamless Non-Twistable, and Non-Twistable variants. Twistable Waveguide horn antennas have a waveguide with a flexible joint, allowing for mechanical adjustment of the horn's shape. Seamless Non-Twistable horns, on the other hand, lack any joints or flex points, providing greater stability and durability. Lastly, Non-Twistable horn antennas are similar to Seamless Non-Twistable ones but may have slight variations in construction. These different horn antenna types cater to various applications and provide options for users based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
