<p><p>The automatic glue machine market can be categorized into three types: hot melt dispenser, water-based glue dispenser, and others. Hot melt dispensers are machines that heat solid glue and dispense it in a liquid form for bonding applications. Water-based glue dispensers, as the name suggests, dispense water-based adhesives for various bonding needs. The "others" category includes glue machines that use different types of adhesives like solvent-based or pressure-sensitive adhesives. Each type of machine caters to specific application requirements and provides efficient and automated glue dispensing solutions.</p></p>
