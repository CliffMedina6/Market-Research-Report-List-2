<p><p>Agricultural dyestuffs are used to add color to various agricultural products. They come in two main types: solution and powder. Solution dyestuffs are pre-dissolved in a liquid form, making them easy to apply through spraying or dipping methods. Powder dyestuffs, on the other hand, are solid particles that need to be mixed with a liquid before application. Both types offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of farmers, such as ease of application, color intensity, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
