<p><p>Pressure transmitters and transducers are devices used to measure pressure in various industrial applications. There are several types of pressure transmitters and transducers available in the market. Piezoresistive pressure transducers utilize the change in resistance of a material under pressure to measure pressure levels. Piezoelectric pressure transducers convert pressure into an electrical charge by using the piezoelectric effect. Other types of pressure transmitters and transducers may include capacitive, optical, or resonant devices. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for specific applications depending on factors such as accuracy, durability, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
