<p><p>Compressor seals are essential components used in compressors to prevent the leakage of gases or fluids. There are two main types of compressor seals: contact seals and non-contact seals. Contact seals rely on direct physical contact between the sealing surfaces to create a barrier against leakage. On the other hand, non-contact seals utilize air or gas cushioning to maintain separation between the surfaces, enabling a contactless sealing mechanism. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and reliability of compressors in various industries.</p></p>
