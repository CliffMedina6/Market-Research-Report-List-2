<p><p>The multiple zone VAV system market consists of two main types: single-duct and dual-duct systems. In a single-duct system, a single supply duct delivers air to multiple zones, with each zone having its own terminal unit for temperature control. On the other hand, a dual-duct system uses two separate supply ducts, one for hot air and one for cold air, to provide precise temperature control in each zone. These systems are commonly used for commercial buildings, providing energy-efficient heating and cooling solutions.</p></p>
