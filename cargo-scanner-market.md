<p><p>There are various types of cargo scanners in the market, each utilizing different technologies for inspecting cargo. Gamma-Ray Radiography Technology employs gamma rays to create images of the cargo, providing detailed information about the contents. X-Ray Radiography Technology uses X-rays to produce images, effectively detecting concealed items in cargo. Muon Tomography Technology, on the other hand, relies on cosmic-ray muons to generate images, allowing for accurate inspection of dense materials. The Muon Tomography Technology market refers to the industry surrounding the use and advancement of this specific technology for cargo scanning purposes.</p></p>
