<p><p>The electrolyte solution market consists of two types - liquid lithium-ion battery electrolyte and solid lithium-ion battery electrolyte. Liquid lithium-ion battery electrolyte is a type of solution that enables the movement of ions between the battery's electrodes, providing a conductive medium for the battery's operation. Solid lithium-ion battery electrolyte, on the other hand, is a non-liquid solution that functions similarly but offers the advantage of enhanced safety and stability. These electrolyte solutions are vital components for lithium-ion batteries, facilitating their efficient functioning and power storage capabilities.</p></p>
