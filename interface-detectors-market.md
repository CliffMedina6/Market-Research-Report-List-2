<p><p>Interface detectors, also known as interface level gauges, are devices used to determine the level of an interface that separates two immiscible liquids, typically oil and water. Firstly, the increasing demand for oil and gas, coupled with the exploration and production activities in offshore fields, is driving the need for accurate interface level measurement.
