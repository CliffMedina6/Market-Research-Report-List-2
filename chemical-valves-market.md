<p><p>The chemical valves market includes various types of valves used in chemical industries. Gate valves are used to regulate flow by raising or lowering a gate, while control valves precisely modulate flow rates. Ball valves have a ball-shaped disc that controls flow by pivoting open or closed. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to regulate flow. Other types of valves used in the chemical industry include check valves, diaphragm valves, plug valves, and pinch valves. These valves help in controlling and directing the flow of chemicals in industrial processes.</p></p>
