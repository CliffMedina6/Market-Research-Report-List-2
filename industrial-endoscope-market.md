<p><ul><li>Fiberscopes</li><li>Rigid Borescopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Endoscope Market encompasses two main types: Fiberscopes and Rigid Borescopes. Fiberscopes are flexible and use fiber optic technology to transmit images, making them suitable for inspecting curved or complex structures. Rigid Borescopes, on the other hand, consist of a solid tube with lenses and provide higher resolutions for detailed inspections in straight, narrow spaces. Both types enable professionals in various industries to visually inspect internal components or areas that are otherwise difficult to access, facilitating maintenance, quality control, and troubleshooting processes.</p></p>
