<p><p>A current feedback amplifier (CFA) is a type of operational amplifier that utilizes a current rather than a voltage feedback loop to enhance the performance of a circuit. In CFAs, the current flowing through the feedback resistor is used to stabilize gain, improve bandwidth, and enhance overall linearity. They are widely used in a variety of applications, including audio and video equipment, telecommunications, medical devices, and instrumentation.</p><p>The current feedback amplifier market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for high-performance amplifiers with wide bandwidth and low distortion is driving the adoption of CFAs.
