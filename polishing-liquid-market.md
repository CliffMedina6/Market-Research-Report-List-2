<p><p>The polishing liquid market consists of various types, including Polycrystalline Diamond, Silicon Oxide, Cerium Oxide, Alumina, and Silicon Carbide. Polycrystalline Diamond polishing liquids utilize tiny diamond particles for precise and efficient polishing. Silicon Oxide liquids are known for their excellent optical properties and are often used for polishing delicate surfaces. Cerium Oxide liquids are used primarily for glass polishing due to their high scratching resistance. Alumina liquids are commonly used for metal polishing, while Silicon Carbide liquids are effective in achieving a smooth finish on hard materials such as ceramics and stones.</p></p>
