<p><strong>The Nomex Honeycomb Core Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hexagonal Cell</li><li>Rectangular(Overexpanded)</li><li>Pentagonal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nomex honeycomb core market is divided into three types based on the shape of the cells. Hexagonal cell honeycomb cores consist of interconnected hexagonal cells, providing excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Rectangular (overexpanded) honeycomb cores have cells that are stretched in one direction, offering increased stiffness. Pentagonal honeycomb cores have pentagonal cells, providing good compressive strength. These different market types cater to various industrial applications where lightweight and high strength materials are needed, such as aerospace, automotive, and construction sectors.</p></p>
