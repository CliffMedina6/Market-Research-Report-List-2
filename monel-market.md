<p><p>Monel is a type of nickel-copper alloy that possesses exceptional corrosion resistance and high strength. Monel 400 is the most commonly used grade, suitable for acid and alkaline environments. Monel 401 is similar but with a higher silicon content for added strength. Monel 404 is specifically designed for welding applications. Monel 405 contains added sulfur to enhance machinability. Monel 450 is a forged alloy recommended for high-temperature applications. Monel K-500 has excellent mechanical properties and is resistant to seawater corrosion. Monel R-405 is a free-machining version of Monel 400, while Monel 502 consists of nickel, copper, iron, and aluminum, offering good resistance to corrosion and oxidation.</p></p>
